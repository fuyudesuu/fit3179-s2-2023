Beyond the Stage: Unveiling the statistics of Valorant Champions 2023

What is VALORANT ?

Valorant is a 5v5 competitive tactical shooter developed by Riot Games and released in June 2020. The release has taken the gaming community by storm and been growing ever since. Players will play as both Defenders and Attackers, with different objectives on each side and swap after half game. Its diverse character (also known as Agents) system with unique abilities is the core factor of the game and it has pushed players to develop sophisticated strategies other than just mechanical skill to lead themselves to victory.

About VALORANT Champions Tour

Valorant Champions Tour is a global competitive tournament series organized by Riot Games. During the year, franchise teams from different regions will compete in their respective domestic league and accumulate circuit points to qualify for one international tournament, VALORANT Masters, and one final international tournament, VALORANT Champions.

At Champions, 16 qualified teams from 4 major regions: Americas, EMEA, Pacific, China will then compete to prove that they are the best. Viewership during international tournaments period can peak above 1 million viewers concurrent on Twitch.tv platform alone.

Which maps are being played at Champions 2023?

At top level competition, every decisions matter, from Agent selections to Map Ban/Pick decisions. These decisions can become a huge counterplay to opponents' pregame planning and strategies

Here we can see in terms of most ban maps, Fracture tops the rank, following up by Haven. Therefore, because of its popular ban, these 2 maps are less likely to be picked or played.

However, these 2 maps also have one of the more balanced Win Rate for both Defender and Attackers.

On the other hand, Split takes the cake for most picked map throughout the tournament, while other maps are almost equally picked.

Furthermore, it is important for developers to address the issue unbalanced advantages of two sides on a map to maintain highest competitive integrity, which can be seen happening on Split and Lotus with higher win rate on Attackers, while Defenders side of Bind has a huge advantage over Attackers


Player Performance throughout tournament

Evil Geniuses emerged victorious against Paper Rex ultimately at the Grand Finals. However, how are players in each team perform throughout the tournament? Are they underperforming? Or perhaps Evil Geniuses players stepped up their game despite coming in as a dark horse of the tournament?

Most Evil Geniuses players have great performance statistics but not necessarily significantly excel than other players, showing how low the skill difference are at the highest level of competition

Note on statistics used:

Impact Score: This is calculated from various factors in game such as Economy management, Kills, Assists, Clutch Rounds,...

Clutch%: Percentage of rounds won as the last standing of the team.
